Push the limits of Homos Sapiens and save all who suffer!

Dianbo Liu is interested in science , philosophy and hiking. In his spare time, he practices stand-up comedy. As a scientist Dianbo is working on 1) consciousness inspired machine learning and 2) apply machine learning to answer biomedical questions. Dianbo Liu works at Broad institute of MIT and Harvard as a group leader in machine learning for health, focusing on applying advanced machine learning methods to improve personalization of medicine. Prior to Broad, Dianbo Liu was a postdoc working with Prof. Yoshua Benio ( Turing award winner) on conciousness inspired machine learning after his study at University of Dundee, MIT and then Harvard.
